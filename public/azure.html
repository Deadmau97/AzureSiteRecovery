Estimates only — please read

This Azure Calculator returns cost estimates derived from the public Azure Retail Prices API. The numbers reflect list prices at the moment they were fetched and ignore:

  • Customer / EA / CSP / partner discounts you may have
  • Actual workload usage patterns (uptime, autoscale, burst)
  • Bandwidth and operations outside the line items shown
  • Currency conversion fluctuation and taxes
  • Programmes such as Azure Hybrid Benefit beyond what you toggle in the UI

Real invoices may differ materially. Always cross-check with the official Azure Pricing Calculator and your Microsoft account team before committing.
